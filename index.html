Prepare

Enter into stillness

  • Settle into a comfortable position. Take a moment to quiet your body and mind.
  • Call to mind God's presence. Enter the quiet space in your heart where the Lord lives, and be still.
  • Sit in the gaze of the Father as He looks upon you with love. Respond to His gaze with openness.
  • Imagine Jesus sitting beside you: present, listening, attentive, and loving.

"Lord, help me to see myself as You see me. Help me to know myself as You know me. Grant me the grace to recognize Your Will throughout my day — the times we worked together, and the times I failed You."

Take a moment to pause before continuing

Reflect

Examine your day

  • Think through your day, moment by moment.
  • Allow the Lord to reveal aspects of your day that He would like to look at with you.
  • Notice any missed opportunities or times you could have behaved differently.
  • Call to mind your sins.
  • Sit in the gaze of the Father as He looks upon you with love. Respond to His gaze with openness.
  • Ask the Lord for His forgiveness. Feel His mercy and peace filling you.

"O my God, I am heartily sorry for having offended you, and I detest all my sins because I dread the loss of Heaven and the pains of hell. But most of all, because I have offended You, My God, who are all good and deserving of all my love. I firmly resolve with the help of Your grace to confess my sins, do penance and amend my life. Amen"

Take a moment to pause before continuing

Review

Converse with God

  • Tell God about the things that stood out as you reflected on your day. Did they draw you closer to God or push you away? Pay attention to your emotions.
  • Allow the Spirit to draw your focus to one specific moment.
  • What did you feel during that moment earlier today? How do you feel about it now? Where was the Lord in that moment? What is He calling you to now?
  • Consider the following questions about your day overall: Where did you feel joy? What challenges you? What troubled you? Where did you see God moving? What would you like to do differently tomorrow?

Take a moment to pause before continuing

Close with Prayer

Gracious and holy Father,
grant us the intellect to understand You, reason to discern You, diligence to seek You, wisdom to find You, a spirit to know You, a heart to meditate upon You.
May our ears hear You, may our eyes behold You, and may our tongues proclaim You.
Give us grace that our way of life may be pleasing to You, that we may have the patience to wait for You and the perseverance to look for You.
Grant us a perfect end — Your holy presence, a blessed resurrection, and life everlasting.
We ask this through Jesus Christ, our Lord.

— St. Benedict
